"I WILL SLEEP AGAIN"

When death awaits me
And the stars would call
Would you come to meet me
The person I lived for

When breathe restrain my words
And silence has gloved
Whisper in my ears that i mattered
Tell me that i was loved

Remember how you treated my anxiety
Answer to all my pain
Would you give me one last smile
And i will sleep again
